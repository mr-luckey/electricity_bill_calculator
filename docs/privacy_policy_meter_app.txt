PRIVACY POLICY
Meter App – Pakistan Electricity Bill Calculator

Effective date: July 18, 2026

================================================================================
1. INTRODUCTION
================================================================================

Welcome to Meter App ("App", "we", "us", or "our"), operated by Skypion Tech.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information
when you use Meter App on Android or iOS.

Meter App helps users estimate residential electricity bills for major Pakistani
distribution companies (DISCOs) based on publicly available tariff data. By using
the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use
the App.

================================================================================
2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
================================================================================

2.1 Account & authentication information

Depending on how you sign in, we may collect:

- Google Sign-In: name, email address, and Google account identifier provided by Google.
- Email authentication: email address and authentication credentials managed by Firebase Authentication.
- Guest / anonymous sign-in: a temporary anonymous user ID (no name or email required).

2.2 App usage & calculation data

- Selected DISCO (electricity company) and consumer category.
- Units (kWh) entered for bill estimates.
- Estimated bill breakdown and related calculation history stored on your device.
- App preferences such as language and theme.

2.3 Device & technical information

- Device type, operating system, and app version.
- Crash logs and performance diagnostics (via Firebase Crashlytics).
- Basic analytics events (via Firebase Analytics), such as app opens and feature usage.
- Advertising identifiers used by Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) for ad delivery and measurement.

2.4 Information we do not collect

- We do not collect your official electricity bill PDF or meter photos.
- We do not require your DISCO consumer number, CNIC, or payment card details to use the calculator.
- Bill estimates are informational only and are not linked to your utility account.

================================================================================
3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
================================================================================

We use the information described above to:

- Provide and improve bill estimation features.
- Create and manage your account (including guest sessions).
- Save calculation history and preferences on your device.
- Sync limited profile data (such as display name and login method) when you are signed in.
- Show advertisements (banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads).
- Monitor app stability, fix bugs, and understand feature usage.
- Respond to feedback or support requests you send us.
- Comply with legal obligations where applicable.

================================================================================
4. LOCAL STORAGE
================================================================================

Meter App stores certain data locally on your device using technologies such as
SharedPreferences and Hive, including:

- Language and theme settings.
- Last selected DISCO and category.
- Recent bill calculation history.
- Session-related preferences.

Uninstalling the App or clearing app data typically removes this local information.
